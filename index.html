Features PrismClimbing

Free Routesetting Easy to use Quick updates

PrismClimbing is 100% free to use. Not only for climbers but also for gyms who want to document their routesetting.

Routesetters can post their new routes with information like grades, color and the author. The climbers can then for example mark them as climbed.

The PrismClimbing website is extremely easy and compact, however anyone could use it and still be satisfied. An experienced tech engineer to cavemen who just came out of their grotto

PrismClimbings databases are built on google's Firebase. A database that is extremely quick and can reach out to hundreds of users at once. This is what makes updating so fast.
